# Literature Screening Process

Step 1: 1,984 references were found after a preliminary search of four databases. 
Step 2: After the initial search in IEEE Xplore, the publication type was restricted to journal articles. For ScienceDirect, only review articles and research articles were selected. In Web of Science, the document types were limited to articles and review articles. No further restriction on publication type was applied in PubMed. A total of 738 references were retained for further processing. During screening, 55 duplicates were identified and removed. The remaining 683 records were imported into Rayyan for title and abstract screening.
Step 3: Each record was independently assessed according to predefined inclusion and exclusion criteria. After preliminary screening and full-text screening, 100 studies were finally included in this review. The screening process is carried out by RAYYAN.
