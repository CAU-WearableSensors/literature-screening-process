# Literature Screening Process

Step 1: 1,889 references were found after a preliminary search of four databases. 
Step 2: After the initial search in IEEE Xplore, the publication type was restricted to journal articles. For ScienceDirect, only review articles and research articles were selected. In Web of Science, the document types were limited to articles and review articles. No further restriction on publication type was applied in PubMed. 702 references were recorded for further processing. Additionally, the screening procedure has been completed. 29 duplicates were found and eliminated. After duplicate removal, the remaining records were imported into Rayyan for title and abstract screening. 673 studies were chosen for eligibility screening as a result.
Step 3: Each record was independently assessed according to predefined inclusion and exclusion criteria. After preliminary screening and full-text screening, 98 studies were finally included in this review. The screening process is carried out by RAYYAN.
